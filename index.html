At a glance

We believe by building transparent bridges between our friends,

and making donations easy, fun and rewarding,

together we could achieve a collaborative, sustainable society

where charities, the planet and YOU (as the Yell Of Unity) win!

Us

We are here to improve collaboration among participants of the charity sector in the United Kingdom and to inspire people to actively participate in tackling pressing challenges which are drifting our world and its inhabitants into crises. Whether you do that on your laptop or by volunteering, offering financial support or expertise, acting globally or locally; the point is that we need to act now.

You

To achieve our noble goals, we need your active participation: your thoughts, your opinion, your creativity, your dedication, your passion. You have all it takes to make a difference. Your voice matters the most, so let us amplify it for you.

Together

There are two key changemakers in our mission.: charities working tirelessly to better people’s lives with the utmost dedication, and extraordinary people like YOU, who want to make a difference, and have fun doing it. Together we are building a community that leverages the power of story and technology to transform our lives. Our model offers everyone the chance to have a once-in-a-lifetime experience co-created with these great organisations. We intend to share these stories with the rest of the world to invite them on an educational journey and mobilise them to become actively involved.

Our Core Values

  • Absolute transparency

    We understand the confusion when people question where their donations go. We believe that the money should go where it can make the most impact. Therefore, ECHO hand-picks its partners who represent the same level of transparency as we do. But in exchange we expect that from you. too.

  • Easy, fun and rewarding donations

    There are many organisations working to make a difference, but it’s hard for them to raise money in a sustainable way. Our goal is to make giving fun and rewarding, so everyone wins. You get the chance to live your dream experience and charities can spend less time fundraising, and more time making an impact.

  • Unique storytelling

    A good story well told has the power to open us to new ways of thinking, give us insight into the experience of others, and ultimately make us more connected. The more connected we are, the more impact we can make together.

  • Cutting-edge video content

    We intend to use compact video content embedded in the ways charities and social enterprises operate and convey information to the public. We are also pioneers in creating a video sharing forum for our members as the most innovative, efficient and transparent way of exchanging thoughts and personal experience.

  • Equal friends

    We strive to create strong bonds with our partners and members, so it is more like a friendship than anything else. We welcome anyone from anywhere with any background; we believe that we are all born equal and everyone has to be treated this way.

  • Powerful, bite-sized knowledge

    We realise that important, life-changing information circulates in certain “bubbles” and remain there without being shared or transferred correctly to the public. We are here to pop those “bubbles” and let the information flow in its pure, easily understandable form.
