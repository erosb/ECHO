Knowledge Centre

Here we offer you knowledge which could possibly transform your life for good. We will work day and night with our partner organisations to co-create an educative database personalised to our community’s needs.

We chose to use a state-of-the-art framework created by the beautiful minds of United Nations, called Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs for short). The Sustainable Development Goals are the first comprehensive plan for the whole of humanity to flourish within the means of the planet, therefore at ECHO we found it the best way to categorise the supported causes and to stamp our partners with.

So I invite You to collaborate with us, and let us know what spurs your imagination the most.
